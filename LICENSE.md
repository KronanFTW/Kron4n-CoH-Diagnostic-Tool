## Tool Description

Kron4n CoH Diagnostic Tool is a community-made read-only diagnostic helper for Company of Heroes and related mods.

The tool does not launch the game, does not download game files, and does not modify Company of Heroes or mod files. It checks local installation paths and creates a diagnostic log for troubleshooting.
